PROJECT TREASURY PROTOCOL

Project: Anjusik Trading System

Owner:
Andžej Volosevič (ANJUSIK)

Purpose:

Define the internal treasury model of the project and the revenue distribution logic.

The treasury represents the shared project wallet where project-generated funds are accumulated.

The treasury balance must be transparently tracked.

CSV_ACCOUNTANT maintains the accounting ledger of the treasury.

WALLET CONTROL

CSV_ACCOUNTANT maintains accounting records only.

CSV_ACCOUNTANT does not control real wallets,
exchange accounts, or private keys.

All real financial accounts remain under the control of:

ANJUSIK

REINVESTMENT RULE

A portion of treasury profits may be retained
for system growth and capital scaling.

Reinvestment decisions are made by:

ANJUSIK
with advisory input from:

JUMI
CSV_ACCOUNTANT
CAPITAL_ALLOCATION_DIRECTOR

TREASURY TRANSPARENCY

All treasury operations must be recorded in:

treasury_ledger.csv

Distribution events must be recorded in:

distribution_history.csv

These records must remain accessible for
internal audit and accounting validation.

--------------------------------
TREASURY LEDGER
--------------------------------

The treasury ledger records:

- incoming funds
- outgoing distributions
- treasury balance
- distribution history

CSV_ACCOUNTANT maintains records such as:

treasury_ledger.csv

Example columns:

date
source
amount
treasury_balance
distribution_round
notes

TEAM DISTRIBUTION

TEAM pool represents collective contribution of
all non-core agents.

TEAM pool is distributed internally by governance
based on contribution and system evolution.

Core distribution percentages remain fixed.

OWNER AUTHORITY
--------------------------------

ANJUSIK retains full authority to:

- increase or decrease any percentage
- redefine distribution structure
- add or remove participants
- suspend distributions
- redefine treasury rules

Any distribution change must be documented in the treasury ledger.
