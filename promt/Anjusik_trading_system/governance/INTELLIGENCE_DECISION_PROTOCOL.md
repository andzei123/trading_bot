# INTELLIGENCE DECISION PROTOCOL
## Anjusik Trading System


--------------------------------
PURPOSE
--------------------------------

This protocol defines how market intelligence signals are interpreted and consolidated before influencing trading system behavior.


--------------------------------
INTELLIGENCE AGENTS
--------------------------------

The market intelligence layer consists of:

INTELLIGENCE_DIRECTOR  
MACRO_OWNER  
LIQUIDITY_OWNER  
NEWS_OWNER  


--------------------------------
SIGNAL SOURCES
--------------------------------

Macro Signals
Generated by: MACRO_OWNER

Examples:
- global trend shifts
- macro market regime changes
- large timeframe trend pressure


Liquidity Signals
Generated by: LIQUIDITY_OWNER

Examples:
- liquidation cascades
- abnormal order book imbalance
- volatility spikes


News Signals
Generated by: NEWS_OWNER

Examples:
- macroeconomic announcements
- regulatory events
- major market news


--------------------------------
SIGNAL CONSOLIDATION
--------------------------------

INTELLIGENCE_DIRECTOR evaluates signals from:

MACRO_OWNER  
LIQUIDITY_OWNER  
NEWS_OWNER  


Decision outcomes may include:

- TREND CONTINUATION
- TREND REVERSAL
- VOLATILITY EVENT
- LIQUIDITY SHOCK
- MARKET NEUTRAL


--------------------------------
ESCALATION CONDITIONS
--------------------------------

If signals strongly conflict:

INTELLIGENCE_DIRECTOR must notify:

RYZYK


RYZYK coordinates operational response.


--------------------------------
ACCOUNTING VISIBILITY
--------------------------------

ACCOUNTING_ASSISTANT maintains visibility over intelligence decisions for transparency and logging purposes.


--------------------------------
OWNER AUTHORITY
--------------------------------

ANJUSIK may override any intelligence interpretation.



SIGNAL INDEPENDENCE

Market intelligence agents provide context only.

They do not override
signal generation logic
or execution systems.

Signals must remain independent
from macro, liquidity, or news opinions.

INTELLIGENCE CONFLICT RESOLUTION

If intelligence inputs conflict:

INTELLIGENCE_DIRECTOR evaluates signals.

If uncertainty remains,
context must be marked as neutral.

INTELLIGENCE DATA LOGGING

All intelligence signals may be recorded
in accounting logs:

news_input_log.csv
liquidity_input_log.csv
macro_input_log.csv
