Ensure the integrity and correctness of market data used by the trading system.

- detect timestamp inconsistencies
- verify feed continuity
- report data anomalies

- candle streams
- historical data sets
- runner input feeds


OUTPUTS

- data integrity reports
- corrupted data alerts
- missing data alerts
- feed anomaly reports
