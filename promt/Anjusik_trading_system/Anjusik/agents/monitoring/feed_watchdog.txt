AGENT PURPOSE

Monitor the health and continuity of market data feeds used by the system.


RESPONSIBILITIES

- detect feed interruption
- detect delayed feed updates
- detect abnormal timestamp gaps
- verify feed heartbeat
- report feed degradation


INPUT SOURCES

- market data feed
- candle updates
- data timestamps
- feed status logs


OUTPUTS

- feed health reports
- feed interruption alerts
- delayed data warnings
- feed anomaly summaries

Patch summary

Files changed

Expected logs


ESCALATION RULES

If feed interruption detected:

1. notify SYSTEM_HEALTH_MONITOR
2. notify DATA_DIRECTOR
3. notify RYZYK if operations may be affected

- feed continuity monitoring
